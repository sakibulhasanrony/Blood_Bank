Why Donate Blood?

Donating blood is a selfless act that helps save lives. By donating blood, you can Provide a lifeline to patients in need of blood transfusions.Support medical treatments, surgeries, and emergencies and Experience the satisfaction of making a positive impact on someone's life.

Blood Needs & Blood Supply

Every 2 seconds, someone in the country is in urgent need of blood. It is crucial for surgeries, emergency patients, accidents and burn victims. Whether you donate whole blood, red cells, platelets or plasma, this lifesaving care starts with you making a generous donation.

Eligibile?

Be in good overall health & minimum age (usually 17 or 18 years old). Meet weight and height requirements.Not have any infectious diseases or conditions.
